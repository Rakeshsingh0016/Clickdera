Our Story & Team

Founded on July 8th, 2025 in Jitali, Ankleshwar, Bharuch, Gujarat by Rakesh Kumar Singh, Clickdera was born from a vision to revolutionize digital marketing services. After working unofficially with 36+ brands, Rakesh identified a critical industry problem - agencies were overcharging clients while delivering subpar results.

Determined to solve this, he established Clickdera with a mission to provide premium-quality services at affordable rates. Our agency helps businesses grow faster by eliminating unnecessary costs while maintaining exceptional service quality.
