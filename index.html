About Us

Learn more about our mission and what drives us to deliver exceptional results

Performance-Driven Digital Marketing

Clickdera was founded by Rakesh Kumar Singh on 17th July 2025 with a clear mission — to deliver high-quality digital marketing services at affordable prices.

After collaborating unofficially with over 36 brands, Rakesh noticed a common issue in the industry. Many agencies were overcharging clients while failing to deliver quality leads or meaningful results. This often led to wasted budgets and frustration among business owners.

To solve this problem, he started Clickdera — an agency built on transparency, performance, and value. From affiliate marketing to website design, influencer campaigns to reputation management, our goal is simple: provide effective services that help businesses grow without overspending.

At Clickdera, you don't just spend — you grow.
